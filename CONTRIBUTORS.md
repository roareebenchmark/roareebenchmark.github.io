Members are added to this list after:
1. Completing the vetting process
2. Being granted repository access
3. Making their first contribution

To update your entry, submit a PR with your information following this format:

```markdown
### [Your Name]
- **Affiliation:** Columbia University, [Department/Program]
- **Research Interests:** [Brief description]
- **Contributions:** [Brief description of contributions]
- **GitHub:** [@username](https://github.com/username)
```
